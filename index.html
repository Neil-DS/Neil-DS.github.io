Tayside Re-users

A simple python application using tkinter to processing data for a reuse charity. The charity recycled old items and saved them from being discarded and kept track of their impact by estimating the amount of CO2 they were saving from being outputted into the atmosphere. The application takes a list of the items that were sold on that week and checks against a list to see if it's holds the information about the amount of CO2 that would be saved along with the items weight, and ID code.
